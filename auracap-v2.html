CBAM Jan 2026
CBAM enters definitive phase — full financial obligations begin January 2026
The EU's Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism moved from reporting-only transitional phase into its definitive regime on 1 January 2026. Importers of cement, steel, aluminium, fertilizers, electricity and hydrogen must now purchase CBAM certificates reflecting embedded carbon emissions. Regulation (EU) 2025/2083 introduced key simplifications including a 50-tonne de minimis threshold exempting smaller importers, while keeping 99% of emissions in scope.
